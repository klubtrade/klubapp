## 1. Who we are

KLUB ("KLUB," "we," "us," "our") is a front-end interface operated by KLUB Labs, \[jurisdiction TBD\]. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we process it, and your rights with respect to that data.

For purposes of the EU General Data Protection Regulation ("GDPR"), the data controller is KLUB Labs. For purposes of the California Consumer Privacy Act ("CCPA"), the business is KLUB Labs.

Contact: privacy@klub.trade

## 2. What we collect

We distinguish between three categories:

### 2.1 Data you provide directly
- **Email address** — captured at waitlist signup, invite redemption, and account creation.
- **Chosen handle** — a pseudonymous username you select. Not required to be your real name.
- **Account credentials** — if you sign up via email + password. We never see your password in plaintext; we store a salted, hashed form using industry-standard key-derivation (argon2id).
- **Communication content** — anything you send us via email, support form, or in-app feedback.

### 2.2 Data generated by your use of the Service
- **Wallet addresses** — public Solana or Bulk Net addresses you connect. These are inherently public on-chain data.
- **Agent Wallet metadata** — scope, notional caps, expiry, and last-used timestamp for each Agent Wallet you authorize. No private keys are stored; private keys live only in your wallet.
- **Trade journal entries** — entry reasoning and post-trade reflections you type into Practice mode. These are yours and we treat them as sensitive.
- **Copy-trade relationships** — which Leaders you follow, your allocation caps, stop overrides, and pause states.
- **Alert preferences** — which alert channels you've enabled (push, email, Telegram) and which thresholds.

### 2.3 Data we collect automatically
- **Log data** — IP address, user agent, timestamps, requested URLs, and referrer. Used for security, abuse prevention, and troubleshooting.
- **Device identifiers** — for push notifications and session management.
- **Geolocation (IP-derived)** — inferred country-level location, used solely to enforce geoblocking of Restricted Jurisdictions (see Terms of Service §3).
- **Cookies and local storage** — session tokens, theme preferences, trade journal (client-side only for V1). A separate Cookie Notice lists every specific cookie and its purpose.

## 3. Data we do NOT collect

We want to be explicit about what we don't touch:

- **Your wallet private keys.** Never. They live in your wallet, we never request them, we never transmit them.
- **Plaintext passwords.** Hashed only.
- **Financial instrument data beyond what's public on-chain.** We read your on-chain positions through the Bulk API using the access you authorize; we don't have private backend access to your balance beyond that.
- **Biometric identifiers.** We don't use facial recognition or fingerprint scanning.
- **Children's data.** The Service is not directed to anyone under 18 and we do not knowingly collect data from minors.

| Purpose | Legal basis (GDPR) | Examples |

| Providing the Service | Contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) | Letting you log in; executing copy-trade mirrors you've authorized |
| Security and fraud prevention | Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) | Detecting credential stuffing; blocking sanctioned addresses |

If we ever process data for a purpose not covered above, we will update this policy and, where required, request your consent before processing.

## 5. Who we share your data with

We are a small team and we do not sell your data. Data is shared only with:

### 5.1 Sub-processors (service providers working on our behalf)
Listed here, with the category of data they process:

- **Cloud infrastructure** (e.g., AWS, Cloudflare, Vercel) — all infrastructure data
- **Email delivery** (Resend) — your email address and message content
- **Customer analytics** (e.g., PostHog self-hosted or similar) — usage events, with IP anonymized
- **Ramp provider** (e.g., Coinbase Onramp) — KYC data is handled by them, not us; we receive only the transaction outcome
- **Error reporting** (e.g., Sentry) — stack traces, potentially including your user ID but not sensitive data

A full list of current sub-processors is maintained at \[URL TBD\] and is updated whenever we add or change providers.

### 5.2 Law enforcement and regulators

### 5.3 Business transfers
If KLUB Labs is acquired or sells substantially all of its assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. The acquirer would be bound by commitments no less protective than this policy.

### 5.4 What we do NOT share
- We do not sell your data to data brokers or advertisers.
- We do not share your data with third parties for their independent marketing purposes.
- We do not share your trade journal entries with anyone.

## 6. On-chain data

This needs a separate call-out because it's where a lot of "privacy" confusion in crypto comes from.

**Data that is inherently public on-chain:**
- Your wallet addresses
- Every trade you place on Bulk through KLUB
- Every deposit and withdrawal
- Every Agent Wallet authorization and revocation

KLUB does not control this data, cannot redact it, and cannot prevent others from associating your wallet address with your pseudonymous handle or your real-world identity if you've disclosed the link elsewhere (e.g., by tweeting your wallet address). On-chain privacy is an inherent limitation of transparent public blockchains.

If your privacy threat model requires you to keep a wallet address unlinked from you personally, we recommend using a wallet that has not been linked to any KYC'd on-ramp and not signing in with an email you've used elsewhere.

## 7. How long we keep data

- **Account data** (email, handle, preferences): retained while your account is active and for 12 months after deletion, for security and fraud prevention.
- **Log data**: 90 days, then aggregated and anonymized.
- **Trade journal entries**: retained while your account is active; deleted on your request or account deletion.
- **Waitlist emails**: retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion.
- **Communications** (support tickets): 3 years for reference and auditability.

We may retain data longer where required by law (e.g., to respond to sanctions screening or legal process).

## 8. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Access** — get a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Correction** — ask us to fix inaccurate data
- **Deletion** — ask us to delete your personal data (subject to retention requirements above and legal-hold obligations)
- **Portability** — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Objection** — object to processing based on our legitimate interests
- **Restriction** — ask us to pause certain processing
- **Withdrawal of consent** — for any processing based on your consent

**EU/UK residents** have these rights under GDPR/UK-GDPR. **California residents** have analogous rights under CCPA/CPRA, plus the right to know what "personal information" we've sold (none) or shared for cross-context behavioral advertising (none).

To exercise any of these rights, email **privacy@klub.trade**. We will respond within 30 days (or as required by applicable law). You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in your country of residence.

## 10. Security

We take security seriously. Measures include:

- TLS 1.2+ for all network traffic
- Encryption at rest for databases
- Hardware-backed key management for any cryptographic material we hold (which is very little — see §3)
- Least-privilege access controls for engineering
- Regular third-party security audits and penetration testing once funded
- Incident response process for data breaches, with notification to affected Users and regulators as required by law

No system is perfectly secure. If you believe your account or data has been compromised, email **security@klub.trade**.
