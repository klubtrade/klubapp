Copy the posts as-is or re-voice them. Whatever brand voice lands on, **keep it**. Consistency is the entire asset.

## Tone notes

- **First person plural.** "We're building," not "The team at KLUB…"
- **No rocket emojis, no gm spam, no purple prose.** We're not yet another crypto app.
- **Numbers are specific.** "60% get liquidated in month one" beats "most lose money."
- **Short paragraphs, long confidence.** One idea per post, committed to fully.
- **Amber is the brand color.** When you post a screenshot, the amber is what you want people to remember.
