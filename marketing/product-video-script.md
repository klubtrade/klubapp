# KLUB Product Video — 90-Second Hero

> Landing-page hero video. Screen record the product, VO over the top, no faces, no music on the first cut.

**Total runtime:** 88s
**Voice:** first person, founder, conversational. Not "narrator."
**Cadence:** ~130 words/min. Breathe. Don't rush the product.

## Shot list (for the screen record pass)

You'll record the product twice — once clean (no cursor), once with cursor and clicks — and cut between them in editing.

Clean pass (for establishing shots):
1. Landing page, full page scroll top-to-bottom (15s)
2. Home dashboard, load and rest (5s)
3. Quick Trade, fresh load with math summary visible (5s)
4. Follow leaderboard, page load (5s)
5. Leader profile, page load (5s)
6. Basis page, APY hero visible (5s)
7. Desk page, funding table ticking (5s)
8. Pro terminal, full grid (8s)

Cursor pass (for interaction shots):
1. Click "Enter the app" on landing
2. Click risk profile card on home
3. Quick Trade: click Up, click BTC, drag slider from 10 → 20%
4. Follow: click top leader
5. Leader profile: click "Start following" button (don't complete)
6. Basis: type 500 in deposit field (don't click deposit)
7. Desk: hover a row
8. Pro: press ⌘K, type "ETH", press Enter

## Production notes

- **Resolution:** 1920×1080, 60fps. Downsize to 1280×720 for the landing-page loop.
- **File format:** MP4 (H.264, ~2.5 Mbps target). Keep under 15MB.
- **Silent version:** produce a second cut with no VO, just the screen recording. For autoplay muted on the landing hero.
- **Captions:** burn in on the muted version. Use the VO as caption text.
- **No music in v1.** Stock music dates the video. Add only if specifically tested.

## Why no faces

Everyone's founder video looks the same: founder on camera, coffee shop, "hey I'm X and I built Y." Skip it. The product is the hero. When the product is the hero, the video outlasts the team.

## Placements

- **Landing page** — autoplay muted loop, silent version, 1280×720
- **Pitch emails** — attach the 90s cut with VO
- **Farcaster/Twitter posts** — export 3×15s clips from shots 3, 4, and 8
- **Product Hunt launch** — headline video slot
- **Demo Day / investor calls** — lead with the VO version before talking
